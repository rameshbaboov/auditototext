* **Definition:** Volunteers are teens and adults who choose to spend time helping others in various ways. They typically dedicate 2-3 hours per week.
* **Benefits for the People Helped:** Volunteering can assist those facing physical or behavioral challenges, offering practical help (e.g., cooking, budgeting, shopping) or emotional support (e.g., companionship).
* **Benefits for Volunteers:**  Volunteering helps develop self-reliance and a sense of purpose in life. It can enhance resumes when applying for jobs.
